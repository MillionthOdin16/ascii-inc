---

## Pain Points We Address
1. **"ASCII art doesn't sell"** -- Collectors don't have a dedicated place to buy. Solution: Ascii Inc creates a focused marketplace with buyers pre-qualified for ASCII art.
2. **"My audience can't find me"** -- ASCII artists are scattered across Reddit, DeviantArt, Neocities. Solution: One curated storefront with built-in discovery.
3. **"I don't know what to charge"** -- No price reference for ASCII art. Solution: Tiered pricing framework (small: $15-30, medium: $50-100, large/complex: $150-300).
4. **"Selling digital art is a hassle"** -- Delivery, licensing, payment processing. Solution: Instant digital delivery, automated licensing, Stripe payouts.
5. **"I want feedback from serious collectors"** -- Social platforms are noisy. Solution: Curated community where buyers appreciate the craft.
