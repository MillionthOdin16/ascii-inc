# Collector Onboarding Checklist
*Ascii Inc | Version 1.0 | 2026-04-01*

---

## Why This Checklist

A collector who has a smooth, trustworthy first purchase experience becomes a repeat buyer. This checklist ensures every new collector goes from sign-up to download in under 5 minutes, with a clear understanding of what they're getting and how Ascii Inc protects them.

**Goal:** Make the first purchase feel effortless and trustworthy. Friction kills conversions.

---

## Phase 1: Account Setup (Est. 2 minutes)

### 1.1 Sign Up
- [ ] Visit [asciinc.com](https://asciinc.com) and click "Sign Up"
- [ ] Create an account via email (or Google OAuth for fastest setup)
- [ ] Verify your email address (click the link in your inbox)

### 1.2 Account Preferences
- [ ] Set your display name (how you'll appear to artists you support)
- [ ] Add a profile photo (optional but builds trust with artists)
- [ ] Set your preferred currency (USD default; EUR, GBP available)
- [ ] Opt in/out of the newsletter (recommend: opt in for artist spotlights and new releases)

---

## Phase 2: Discovery (Finding Art You Love)

### 2.1 Browse the Catalog
- [ ] Start on the homepage to see curated collections and featured artists
- [ ] Click "Browse All" to filter by:
  - **Theme:** Characters, Landscapes, Objects, Typography, Animations, Abstract
  - **Size:** Small (under 500 characters), Medium (500-2000), Large (2000+)
  - **Price range:** Use the price filter ($15-$300+)
  - **Style:** Monochrome, Multi-color (ANSI), Retro/terminal, Minimalist
- [ ] Sort by: Newest, Most Popular, Price (low/high), Trending

### 2.2 Search
- [ ] Use the search bar for specific subjects (e.g., "fox", "sunset", "robot")
- [ ] Filter by tags (try `#portrait`, `#nature`, `#retro`, `#minimalist`)
- [ ] Use quotes for exact phrase matching ("terminal sunset")

### 2.3 Evaluate a Listing
Before purchasing, review:
- [ ] The full-size preview (click to expand)
- [ ] Artist bio and previous sales history (shows seller credibility)
- [ ] Description and artist notes (explains the piece, technique, inspiration)
- [ ] Edition info (limited editions show remaining copies)
- [ ] File format details (what you'll receive: .txt, .ans, .png preview, etc.)
- [ ] Size specifications (character count, recommended display size)

## Phase 3: Purchasing (Est. 2 minutes)

### 3.1 Add to Cart

### 3.2 Checkout
- [ ] Click "Checkout"
- [ ] Review order total (price + any applicable taxes)

- [ ] Click "Complete Purchase"

### 3.3 Confirmation
- [ ] Order confirmation page displays immediately
- [ ] Confirmation email sent to your registered address
- [ ] Receipt available in "My Purchases" > "Order History"

## FAQ

**Q: Can I resell ASCII art I purchase?**
A: Per the standard personal use license, resale is not permitted. Commercial licensing is available for some pieces -- check the listing's specific license terms or contact the artist directly.

**Q: What if the artist doesn't deliver my file?**
A: Contact support@asciinc.com with your order number. If the artist doesn't deliver within 24 hours of a confirmed purchase, we issue a full refund.

**Q: How do I know the ASCII art will look good on my screen?**
A: Every listing shows a live preview at actual size. Listings also include character count and recommended display size. ASCII art looks best in a terminal window or any monospace font viewer.

**Q: Can I request a custom commission?**
A: Some artists accept custom commissions. Look for artists who list "Commissions Open" on their profile, or send a direct message through the platform. Custom commissions are handled separately from the marketplace.

**Q: What format will I receive?**
A: ASCII art is typically delivered as a .txt file (raw text). Some artists also include a .ans file (ANSI/colored) and/or a .png preview. File format is listed on each listing page.

**Q: How do I display ASCII art?**
A: The simplest method: open the .txt file in any text editor and set it to a monospace font (Courier, Fira Code, JetBrains Mono). For ANSI art, use PabloDraw (free, macOS/Linux/Windows) or AceText.
