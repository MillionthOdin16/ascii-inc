# Email Capture & Collector Community Building Strategy
*Ascii Inc CMO | 2026-04-01*

---

## Overview

Build a collector email list as the primary owned-channel for driving repeat purchases. Email is the highest-ROI channel for digital art -- collectors who opt in are pre-qualified and more likely to buy. Goal: 200 subscribers in Month 1, 1,000 by Month 3.

---

## 1. Lead Magnets (What Gets People to Subscribe)

### Primary Lead Magnet: "Free ASCII Art Print"
- Offer a free downloadable ASCII art piece from a curated set
- Rotate monthly to keep it fresh
- Landing page: [asciinc.com/free-art]
- Email capture on the page (single opt-in for speed)
- Auto-deliver via email immediately after signup

**Why it works:** Immediate tangible value. No commitment beyond email. Gives the collector a sample of what Ascii Inc offers.

### Secondary Lead Magnets
- **"The ASCII Art Starter Collection"** -- 5 free pieces from different artists (sample, not a full purchase)
- **"ASCII Art for Your Terminal"** -- terminal-themed art optimized for tput/figlet export
- **"How to Display ASCII Art"** -- short guide (PDF) on the best tools and settings to display ASCII art beautifully

### Optional: Social Login
- Google OAuth for fastest signup (reduces friction from email verification)
- Twitter OAuth for artists already connected to the ASCII art community

## 3. Email Sequence for New Subscribers

### Welcome Series (3 emails over 7 days)

**Email 1: Welcome + Lead Magnet Delivery (Immediate)**
- Subject: Your free ASCII art is ready
- Content: Deliver the free piece(s), introduce Ascii Inc, link to browse catalog
- CTA: "Browse the Full Collection"
- Tone: Warm, not salesy. "Here's your art. We'll send you the best new pieces weekly."

**Email 2: Meet the Artists (Day 3)**
- Subject: The artists behind the ASCII
- Content: Spotlight 3 founding artists with their best pieces
- Include: Artist bio, profile link, favorite piece thumbnail
- CTA: "See Their Work"
- Tone: Human interest. Show the craft behind the art.

**Email 3: First Purchase Offer (Day 7)**
- Subject: Something caught your eye?
- Content: Personalized recommendation based on browsing (if available) or "Trending This Week"
- Include: Limited-time offer (e.g., 15% off first purchase, valid 48 hours)
- CTA: "Claim Your Discount"
- Tone: Gentle urgency. "These limited editions won't last."

### Ongoing Newsletter (Weekly, Thursdays)

**Format: "The ASCII Art Weekly"**
- **Top Pick of the Week:** 1 featured piece with artist spotlight
- **New Arrivals:** 3-5 new listings with thumbnail
- **Artist Spotlight:** Quick bio + 1 piece from a new artist
- **Community:** Contest results, trending community posts
- **CTA:** Browse the catalog, follow favorite artists

**Subject line templates:**
- "[Artist Name] just dropped something incredible"
- "This week's best ASCII art (3 min read)"
- "First look: [Theme] collection"
- "The ASCII art that blew up this week"

## 4. Community Presence Strategy

### Reddit
- **r/asciiart (60K+):** Weekly artist spotlight post (genuine, not promotional)
- **r/DigitalArt (2M+):** Monthly "ASCII art appreciation" thread
- **r/retrocomputing (150K+):** ASCII art featuring vintage terminals
- Rule: Always add value. Never just post "buy my art." Share process, context, artist stories.

### Discord
- Join ASCII art Discord servers (textmode, demoscene, art communities)
- Participate genuinely for 2 weeks before mentioning Ascii Inc
- Offer to host AMAs with Ascii Inc artists on existing servers
- Create Ascii Inc Discord server only after 50+ collectors

### Twitter/X
- Share daily ASCII art with credit to artists
- Thread format: "ASCII Art Spotlight: [Artist Name]" with 3-4 pieces
- Engage with ASCII art creators, retweet their work
- Use hashtags: #ASCIIart, #textmode, #TerminalArt

### GitHub
- Open-source an ASCII art viewer/converter as a community tool
- Include Ascii Inc branding and signup link in the README
- Submit to Awesome lists (awesome-ascii-art)

### Hacker News / Dev.to
- Monthly technical blog post: "How ASCII Art Rendering Works", "Building an ASCII Art Marketplace"
- Include Ascii Inc as a reference, not the sole focus
- Newsletter signup link in author bio

## 6. Email Platform

**Recommended: ConvertKit (now Kit)**
- Free tier: up to 10K subscribers, unlimited emails
- Built for creators (good fit for artist/marketplace platform)
- Sequences, tags, and automation included
- $0 until monetization proves the model

**Alternative: Mailchimp** (free up to 500 subscribers, more complex UI)
**Alternative: Resend** (developer-friendly, API-first, $0 for <3K emails/month)
