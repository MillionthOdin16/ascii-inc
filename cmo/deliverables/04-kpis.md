# Top 3 Marketing KPIs
*CMO Report | 2026-04-01*

## KPI Philosophy
We optimize for engaged quality over vanity volume. A sale from a repeat collector is worth more than 100 casual visitors who never return. These three KPIs reflect the health of our flywheel: traffic brings buyers, buyers attract artists, artists create inventory that drives more traffic.

---

## KPI 1: Monthly Active Collectors (MAC)
**Definition:** Unique buyers who completed at least one purchase in a 30-day window.
**Why this matters:** Revenue is the ultimate test of product-market fit. MAC is more meaningful than total registered users because it measures actual purchasing behavior, not just signups.
**Targets:**
- Launch month (Month 1): 10 MAC
- Month 3: 50 MAC
- Month 6: 150 MAC
- Month 12: 500 MAC
**Measurement:** Calculated monthly from sales data. Tracked weekly.
**What influences this:** Buyer acquisition campaigns, product quality, pricing, repeat purchase rate.

---

## KPI 2: Artist Signups (Qualified)
**Definition:** New artists who complete onboarding, upload at least one piece, and set a price within 7 days of signup.
**Why this matters:** A marketplace needs supply before it can drive demand. We track qualified signups (not just email captures) because an artist who never uploads is not contributing to the marketplace.
**Targets:**
- Launch month: 15 qualified artist signups
- Month 3: 40 qualified artist signups
- Month 6: 100 qualified artist signups
- Month 12: 300 qualified artist signups
**Measurement:** Calculated weekly from onboarding data.
**What influences this:** Artist recruitment campaigns, platform features, artist testimonials, commission rates.

---

## KPI 3: Collector Acquisition Cost (CAC)
**Definition:** Total sales and marketing spend divided by new buyers acquired in the same period.
**Why this matters:** We need sustainable unit economics. If our CAC exceeds average order value, we lose money on every new customer. Tracking CAC forces discipline in spend efficiency.
**Targets:**
- Launch phase (Months 1-3): CAC target < $25 (acceptable for building market presence)
- Months 4-6: CAC target < $15
- Months 7-12: CAC target < $10
**Measurement:** Monthly, from ad spend + marketing costs vs new buyer count.
**What influences this:** Channel efficiency (Reddit vs paid ads vs organic), conversion rate optimization, landing page performance.

---

## Secondary KPIs (reported monthly)

**Traffic:**
- Monthly unique visitors: target 500 (Month 1), 2,000 (Month 3)
- Email list size: target 200 (Month 1), 1,000 (Month 3)
- Social followers: Twitter 500 (Month 1), 2,000 (Month 3)

**Engagement:**
- Email open rate: target 35%+ (industry avg is 21%)
- Newsletter click rate: target 8%+ (industry avg is 2.6%)
- Repeat purchase rate: target 25%+ by Month 6

**Artist:**
- Artist retention rate: % of artists with 2+ sales in 90 days
- Artist referral rate: % of new artists who were referred by existing artists

**Revenue:**
- Gross merchandise volume (GMV): total sales in period
- Average order value: target $65+
- Platform take rate: 20% of GMV

---

## Reporting Cadence
- **Weekly:** Traffic, artist signups, MAC (cumulative)
- **Monthly:** Full KPI dashboard, CAC, GMV, AOV, retention
- **Quarterly:** Strategy review with CEO, KPI target recalibration
